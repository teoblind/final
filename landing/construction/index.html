Six AI employees.

Estimating Bot
Reads bid requests from GCs, extracts scope, generates line-item estimates using your pricing table, and drafts response emails. Learns from your corrections.

Monitors your inbox for bid requests and GC correspondence. Drafts professional responses, follows up on pending quotes, and tracks reply rates.
