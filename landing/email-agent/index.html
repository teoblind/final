What the Agent Does

One agent. Six capabilities.

Everything a junior hire would do - but faster, cheaper, and always available.

Email Management
Reads your inbox, drafts responses in your tone, follows up on unanswered threads, and flags anything that needs your attention. Nothing falls through the cracks.
Lead Generation
Finds prospects in your industry using Apollo, enriches contacts with company data, revenue, and decision-maker info. Builds your pipeline while you sleep.
Outbound Campaigns
Sends personalized cold emails at scale - not templates. Each message references the prospect's company, industry, and pain points. Handles replies and follow-ups automatically.
Knowledge Base
Learns from every document, email thread, and meeting transcript you share. Ask it anything about your business and get an instant, sourced answer.
Meeting Intelligence
Joins your calls automatically, transcribes everything, extracts action items with assignees and deadlines, and uploads summaries to your shared drive.
Reports & Analysis
Generates weekly reports, market research, and competitive analysis on autopilot. Drops formatted PDFs in your Drive folder every morning before you wake up.
