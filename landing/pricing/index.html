Frequently asked questions

How long does setup take?
Most customers are live within 24 hours. We schedule a 30-minute onboarding call, load your pricing data, connect your email, and configure your agents. You're up and running by end of day.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Month-to-month billing. No contracts, no cancellation fees. If the agents aren't saving you time and money, you shouldn't be paying for them.
What if the bot makes a mistake on an estimate?
Every estimate goes through your approval queue before it's sent. You review the numbers, adjust anything that looks off, and approve or reject. The bot learns from your corrections and gets more accurate over time. Nothing goes to a GC without your sign-off.
Is my data secure?
Your data is encrypted, isolated per tenant, and never shared with other customers or used to train AI models. Each customer gets their own database, their own agents, and their own Drive folder. We can't see your pricing data or your bids.
Do I need to install anything?
No. Coppice runs in your browser and works on any device - desktop, tablet, or phone. You can add it to your phone's home screen for an app-like experience. No downloads, no IT department needed.
What's the difference between Pilot and Full Platform?
Pilot gives you the three core agents: Estimating Bot, Email Agent, and Meeting Bot - enough to automate your bid response cycle. Full Platform adds Field Reporter, Reporting Engine, and the Hivemind knowledge base, plus unlimited seats and custom agent training. Most customers upgrade within 60 days once they see the ROI.
How many people can use it?
Pilot includes 3 seats (e.g. owner, estimator, office manager). Full Platform has unlimited seats - your entire team, project managers, field supervisors, anyone.
